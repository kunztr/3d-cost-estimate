How do I find my part's volume?
STL File: Go to viewstl.com and upload your file.
SolidWorks Part File: Open part in Solidworks -> Tools -> Mass Properties -> Volume
Autodesk Inventor Part File: Open part in Inventor -> Model -> Right click your part -> iProperties -> Physical -> Volume
